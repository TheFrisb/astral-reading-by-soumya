About Me

I'm Soumya an astrologer, twin mama, and a lifelong seeker who found light through the ancient wisdom of the stars. My journey with astrology didn't begin in textbooks - it began in the quiet, challenging moments of early motherhood, when everything felt uncertain. With two toddlers, sleepless nights, and big life questions, I found myself turning to the planets for answers. And they didn't just answer - they transformed me.

What started as curiosity turned into deep study, mentorship under Astro Arun Pandit, and eventually, a calling. Astrology showed me that timing is sacred, delays have meaning, and even our struggles are part of something bigger. It taught me to trust the process, to embrace discipline, and most importantly to keep learning, unlearning, and evolving.

My Philosophy

At Astral Reading, we believe the stars don't dictate your fate - they illuminate your path. Born from a deeply personal journey of healing, motherhood, and self-discovery, Astral Reading was created to offer clarity and compassion through the timeless wisdom of Vedic astrology.

Our mission is simple: to guide you back to yourself. We blend traditional techniques with a modern, practical approach making astrology relatable, grounded, and empowering. Whether you're facing life transitions, seeking purpose, or simply curious about your soul's blueprint, our readings are designed to help you understand your cosmic rhythm and walk forward with confidence.
